Color Usage Details
Neutral — Text & Surfaces

#111111 is the default text color for all body copy, headings, input values, and labels throughout the app.

#333333 is used for secondary body text — descriptions, helper text, and less-prominent labels.

#445566 appears on muted UI labels such as section subheadings, sub-nav item text on hover, and metadata lines inside cards.

#8899AA is the standard muted color: placeholder text in empty inputs, icon fills when not active, and descriptive text that sits below a primary label.

#D0D8E4 is used for all borders and dividers — input field strokes, card edges, separator lines between sections, and the light rules inside tables.

#F0F3F7 is the hover background for ghost and outline buttons — it gives a subtle filled feel on mouseover without introducing color.

#F8F9FA is the global page background. It also fills section containers, panel backgrounds, and divider-band areas that sit between content regions.

#FFFFFF is reserved for cards, modals, sheet drawers, input field backgrounds, and any surface that needs to stand out clearly from the page background.

Brick Burgundy — CTA Buttons

#7A3828 is the primary action color. It fills all enabled CTA buttons (e.g. "Save Journey", "Add Content"), the chat send button, the logo icon mark, and the "submit" action in add-content modals. It signals the single strongest action available on a given screen.

#9B4831 is the hover state for any button using the brick fill. It is slightly lighter and warmer, giving clear rollover feedback without changing hue.

#FBEADF is the disabled CTA background. Disabled buttons use this fill paired with a brick-colored border at 45% opacity, making the disabled state visually distinct while keeping it recognizable as the same button type.

Teal — Journey Tab & Sidebar

#003135 is the darkest teal, used exclusively as the sidebar background and as the dark end of the logo gradient. It anchors the navigation rail and distinguishes it from the content area.

#007E8C is the core teal accent. It appears as the Journey Name tab's selected border, focus rings on all inputs, hyperlink text, the active indicator on sort tabs, and the border on the journey stages area.

#E0F2F4 is the active background for the Journey Name tab — the tab fills this color when selected, providing a soft teal wash that clearly distinguishes it from the three content tabs.

#F1F9F9 (Inactive / Tinted) does double duty: it is the resting background of the Journey Name tab when not selected, the hover background on sort-filter tabs, the thumbnail tint on journey cards in the workspace view, and the tinted section background inside the Journey canvas (e.g., the stages region).

Product — Orange-Terracotta

#C4845A is the Product tab's accent color. It colors the tab's top border when active, all field labels within the Product canvas, focus outlines on Product-tab inputs, hover states on content cards inside Product, and filled chips on journey cards that indicate Product content is complete.

#F4EAE0 is the Product tab's active background — the tab fills this warm off-white when selected.

#F8F4F1 (Inactive / Tinted) is the Product tab's resting background when not selected, the hover background on Product-context ghost buttons, and the tinted section fill used inside Product canvas panels to visually separate structured content areas from white card backgrounds.

Persona — Purple

#7B5B8E is the Persona tab's accent color. It colors the tab border when active, all field labels and focus outlines within the Persona canvas, the border on the persona image placeholder block, and filled persona chips on journey cards.

#E9E0F4 is the Persona tab's active background.

#F4F1F8 (Inactive / Tinted) is the Persona tab's resting background, the hover background on Persona-context ghost buttons, the background fill of the persona image placeholder container, and the tinted section fill used inside Persona canvas panels. It is also used for the face and body silhouette in the persona avatar SVG illustration.

Brand Kit — Sage Green

#7A8B5A is the Brand Kit tab's accent color. It colors the tab border when active, all field labels and focus outlines within the Brand Kit canvas, and filled Brand Kit chips on journey cards.

#EEF4E0 is the Brand Kit tab's active background.

#F5F8F1 (Inactive / Tinted) is the Brand Kit tab's resting background, the hover background on Brand Kit-context ghost buttons, and the tinted section fill used inside Brand Kit canvas panels.
